Hello there my name is Jimmy Kane, I am 26 year old and currently reside in Bensalem, Pennsylvania where I was born & raised.  I am trying to give back to the community that helped me grow into the man I am today.  Aside from working my day job at Ford Motor Company I try my best to give back to the youth & my community at large.  

### Church
In June of 2024 I was was given the opportunity to serve at my church, [Calvary Chapel of Philadelphia](http://ccphilly.org), in the [Koinonia Café](https://www.restaurantji.com/pa/philadelphia/koinonia-caf-/) during the East Coast Pastors conference.  After that experience I feel in love with it (and gained an appreciation for espresso I didn't know I had in the process), which lead me to continue serving in the cafe weekly.  I help out anywhere I can but I volunteer on Wednesday nights & Sunday mornings for 2 of our 3 services.  Additionally, I have started to discipleship group with a group of Senior High boys.  Each year we will read through a book together that relates to different aspects of the Christian life. I've yearned to give back to my church family and I hope to contribute more in the coming years in any way I can.

### Robotics
As a Bensalem High School student I helped found the [Fightin' Robotic Owls FIRST Robotics Team](https://team5401.org) & I went on the become the program manager of the program.  Since 2023 I have served as a Volunteer Coordinator for the district event hosted by the team.  In this role I help recruit volunteers, fill in any gaps, and try to create an overall positive atmosphere for all guest teams and volunteers.  This past year I have begun to referee an additional event at Centennial High School and I hope to expand my involvement in FIRST Mid-Atlanitic Robotics in the coming years.
